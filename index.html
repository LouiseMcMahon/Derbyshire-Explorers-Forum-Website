--- layout: default title: Derbyshire Explorers Forum ---

8th of July 2023

Hosted by the Technical Speleological Group (TSG) as part of their 50th anniversary celebrations on the 8th of July in Castleton village hall.

The Derbyshire Explorers Forum will be a day of talks from as many local clubs, digging groups, explorers and any one else with an interest in the caves and mines of Derbyshire and Staffordshire.
